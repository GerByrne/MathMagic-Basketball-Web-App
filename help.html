To Play

  • Choose a level Easy, Normal or Hard.
  • Drag the basketball with your chosen answer into the net.
  • 10 points awarded if correct, if incorrect then try again.
  • 60 seconds for each game.
  • The highest scores will be added to the scoreboard.

Good Luck!
